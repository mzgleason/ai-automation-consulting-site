# Site Strategy

## Core positioning

A builder-led AI partner who helps teams and small businesses turn promising ideas into real systems, workflows, and prototypes that drive business outcomes.

## Primary audience

- small businesses that want help operationalizing AI
- founders and operators who have ideas but need help getting to a real system
- teams exploring AI workflows, internal tools, lead qualification, intake automation, support automation, or process automation
- startups or teams that need product + AI framing before they build
- decision makers who know AI matters but are overwhelmed by options

## Goals

- position Mark as a builder who can help small businesses and teams build with AI
- convert qualified visitors into conversations
- show proof through case studies, writing, and offer clarity
- create a repeatable content engine that compounds trust and SEO over time
- support a lightweight, credible consulting business without becoming a maintenance burden

## Conversion goals

- primary: discovery calls, advisory calls, prototype sprint engagements, AI workflow design work, and longer-term build support
- secondary: credibility through projects, writing, and offer clarity

## Visitor journey

1. Land on homepage
2. Understand in under 10 seconds what Mark does
3. See proof that this is real work, not vague capability claims
4. Understand what kinds of businesses or problems are a fit
5. Click into a project, writing piece, or Work With Me page
6. Feel enough trust to start a conversation

## Core promise

I help you figure out what is worth building with AI, shape it into a real system, and get it moving.

## Site scope

- Home
- Projects
- Project detail pages
- Writing
- Writing detail pages
- Work With Me
- Contact
- About

## Secondary pages for later phases

- Case studies landing page
- AI Workflow Design service page
- Prototype Sprint service page
- Advisory + Build Support service page
- Best Fit or Who I help page or section
- Resource pages or SEO landing pages

## Out of scope

- heavy CMS in the first major version
- complex newsletter setup
- advanced search in the first major version
- auth
- client portal
