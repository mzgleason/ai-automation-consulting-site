## Problem

The core issue was not awareness. It was the conversion path between interest and qualified action.

## Approach

The build focused on simplifying the intake path, clarifying options, and connecting the marketing surface to downstream qualification logic.

## Outcome

The resulting product direction created a stronger bridge between top-of-funnel traffic and practical lending conversations.
