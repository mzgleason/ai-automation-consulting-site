---
title: Playbooks
description: Simple, reusable templates for automation, operations, and product decisions.
---

These playbooks are short, practical starting points.

- **Workflow map:** Make the work visible and find where things break.
- **Automation audit:** Choose what to automate without creating new failure modes.
- **Decision doc:** Turn fuzzy bets into crisp choices, ownership, and next steps.

If you want help applying one to a real system, reach out through the contact page.
